Writing the future of romance

Studiogusto is an international communication agency that creates cutting edge projects for the digital and the offline worlds, and also for the space between the two.

What makes Studiogusto works so special and recognizable is the neo-romance behind its approach: creating a bond, diving deep into a client’s dreams, desires and values, and emerging with a unique story to tell. 

In the global market and communication scene, Studiogusto is recognized as a boutique agency focused on excellence, with a rare and upfront view on the fast evolution in the field of digital and new media channels. A value that made the team the perfect partner for those brands working on technology and innovation.
