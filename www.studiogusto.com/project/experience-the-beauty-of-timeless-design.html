The Metaphysics of beauty

We express the beauty that Henge creates by giving life to ideas, words, images, videos and digital assets

Henge loves Made-in-Italy and heightened contrasts, through stylish design and the traditional skill of our artisan masters. Studiogusto, since 2011, has been working on Brand positioning and image through the design and strategic use of communication tools shared with the management: Copywriting, Digital Development, Video, Editorial, Social Media and BTL.

Storytelling

A story full of stories.

We attempt to represent the brand through a simple language which is able to empathically describe the stylistic features of each product and their corresponding creation processes. Our productions accompany and support the brand in its positioning journey on an international level, showing clients the magic essence of experimentation and material research. We talk about places, exceptional raw materials, people and Made in Italy.

Paper Matters

The representation of the brand's universe is enriched through the constant publication of catalogues, books, limited edition booklets, special collections and custom invitations. Last but not least H-life, the digital and paper magazine produced entirely by Studiogusto. A true house organ which offers constant updates on Henge's world, connects the various stakeholders and unveils news and insights through emotional stories.

Henge Digital Ecosystem

H-Web

A digital window wide open on Henge's world where access to products is always immediate thanks to a dedicated menu, present in the header of each section.
